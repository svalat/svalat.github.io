    Beautifull things, my best diving pictures

    Science is nice but our planet is so beautifull. We miss somthing if we do not share about all those nice things we can see during our life. Lets take a short time to look on all those small and essential lifes which are arround us. Let them feed our hearts so we can have beautifull ideas and keep in mind we need to protect them.

    I like scuba diving and took pictures of all those beautifull species we have just at our door in the south of france and in our lakes. Here my best pictures. Hope you will enjoy.

    No need of a reflex to make those pictures, this is just with a 10 year old Lumix TZ5 and a lot of patience.
